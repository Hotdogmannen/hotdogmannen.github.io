TWIZARD GOAP & Dungeon Generation

About Project

I made this little game to learn more about AI, specifically for enemies and dungeon generation. I also wanted to challenge myself by designing the game and creating all the assets on my own. You play as a wizard going through rooms defeating monsters trying to find the way to the next floor. Your objective is to get through as many floors as possible but every floor gets harder and harder.

Dungeon Generation

The rooms are premade leading to a lot of control over the rooms, the algorithm places rooms randomly inside an area. After the rooms have been placed, the algorithm starts to connect doorways with hallways so that every room is accessible and finally finds the room farthest from the starting room and connects an end room.

Goal Oriented Action Planning

An AI technique I had heard of was Goal Oriented Action Planning, instead of using a finite state machine, you give the enemy some goals and then actions it can take to achieve those aforementioned goals. This made it really easy to create new enemies and I wouldn't have to maintain any complex state machines but it could lead to unpredictable behavior. For performance sake i used A* pathfinding to find which actions to perform in order to achieve the goal.
